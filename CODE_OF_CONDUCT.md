# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to collaborate respectfully and keep feedback focused on code and outcomes.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, threats, and publication of private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or ban contributors when behavior harms the project or participants.

For conduct issues, contact maintainers through repository private channels.
For security issues, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
